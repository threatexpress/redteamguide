Red Team Development and Operations Audiobook Release

April 7, 2025 · One min read
Joe Vest
Red Team Operator & Author
James Tubberville
Red Team Operator & Author

We are excited to announce the audiobook release of "Red Team Development and Operations," Zero-Day Edition.

The audiobook was published on Amazon's Audible platform using their AI-narration technology. We were positively surprised at the quality of Audible's AI-generated narration — it works well, especially for quickly absorbing content related to red teaming and cybersecurity.
