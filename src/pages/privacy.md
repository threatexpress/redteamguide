# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 19, 2026

RedTeam.Guide ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to transparency about how we collect and use information when you visit our website at [https://redteam.guide](https://redteam.guide).

## Information We Collect

### Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects:

- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Referring website or search terms
- Browser type, operating system, and device type
- Approximate geographic location (country/region level)
- IP address (anonymized)

Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique visitors. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

For more information on how Google processes this data, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [How Google Uses Data When You Use Our Partners' Sites](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

### Opting Out of Analytics

You can opt out of Google Analytics by:

- Installing the [Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- Using your browser's "Do Not Track" setting
- Blocking cookies in your browser settings

## Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not require user accounts or registration
- We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal information
- We do not use advertising trackers
- We do not sell or share data with third parties beyond Google Analytics

## External Links

Our site contains links to external websites (e.g., Amazon, GitHub, Twitter/X). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## Cookies

We use cookies solely for Google Analytics functionality. No cookies are used for advertising, personalization, or tracking across other websites.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
